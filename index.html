    Hi, I’m Arsh Jafri, a passionate software developer and entrepreneur with a deep love for building things that have real-world impact. My journey into entrepreneurship began with e-commerce arbitrage, where I honed my skills in business strategy and market analysis—skills I now apply to my tech projects.

    Outside of the tech and business worlds, I’m all about staying active and creative. You’ll often find me at our campus rec center, hitting the pavement for a run, or exploring new ways to express myself through graphic design. Music is my constant companion, whether I’m coding, relaxing, or just taking a break.

    Volunteering is also close to my heart. I’ve had the privilege of working with organizations like Tayyab Trust, where I helped raise funds for COVID-19 relief in India, and the Cathedral Soup Kitchen, where I served meals to those in need. These experiences have taught me the importance of giving back and have further fueled my drive to make a difference, both through my work and my actions.

    I’m always eager to connect with like-minded individuals who share a passion for technology, business, or just good conversation. If you’re interested in my professional journey, feel free to check out my resume for more details on my experience and skills. Let’s connect and see how we can collaborate!
